Our Story

We opened in May 2010 and our bread has been loved by everyone in the neighbourhood. We use only selected ingredients and we are committed to trustworthy products.

Croissants are our flagship products which is made from french butter, freshly milled organic flour and long fermented periods.

To server a more delightful experience to our customers, we opened a restuarant next to the bakery in July 2018. Our meals are inspired by the seasons and we use fresh fruits and fresh vegetables from the farmers' market
